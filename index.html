Word of God is Life

Matthew 7:24-27

  1. "Therefore whoever hears these sayings of Mine, and does them, I will liken him to a wise man who built his house on the rock:
  2. and the rain descended, the floods came, and the winds blew and beat on that house; and it did not fall, for it was founded on the rock.
  3. But everyone who hears these sayings of Mine, and does not do them, will be like a foolish man who built his house on the sand:
  4. and the rain descended, the floods came, and the winds blew and beat on that house; and it fell. And great was its fall."

Observations:

  1. Who is speaking in the passage?
    • Jesus
  2. Who are the target audience?
    • Everyone who hears the words of Jesus.
  3. What are the two characters used in the parable?
    • Wise man
    • Foolish man
  4. What are the common things between the wise man and the foolish man?
    • Both are building their houses.
    • The rain descended, the floods came, and the winds blew and beat on their houses.
  5. On what did the wise man built his house>
    • Rock
  6. On what did the foolish man built his house?
    • Sand
  7. What happened to wise man's house?
    • It did not fall.
  8. What happened to foolish man's house?
    • It fell and great was its fall.
  9. Who is the wise man?
    • Who hears the word of God and does it.
  10. Who is the foolish man?
    • Who hears the word of God and does not do it.
  11. Where was this event happened?
    • On a mountain.

Matthew 7:13-23

  1. "Enter by the narrow gate; for wide is the gate and broad is the way that leads to destruction, and there are many who go in by it.
  2. Because narrow is the gate and difficult is the way which leads to life, and there are few who find it.
  3. Because of false prophets, who come to you in sheep's clothing, but inwardly they are ravenous wolves.
  4. You will know them by their fruits. Do men gather grapes from thornbushes or figs from thistles?
  5. Even so, every good tree bears good fruit, but a bad tree bears bad fruit.
  6. A good tree cannot bear bad fruit, nor can a bad tree bear good fruit.
  7. Every tree that does not bear good fruit is cut down and thrown into the fire.
  8. Therefore by their fruits you will know them.
  9. Not everyone who says to Me, 'Lord, Lord,' shall enter the kingdom of heaven, but he who does the will of My Father in heaven.
  10. Many will say to Me in that day, 'Lord, Lord, have we not prophesied in Your name, cast out demons in Your name, and done many wonders in Your name?'
  11. And then I will declare to them, 'I never knew you; depart from Me, you who practice lawlessness!'
